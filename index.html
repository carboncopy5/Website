Island of Grogram

Watercolor, 14.5in x 10.5in: Concept art for Grogram. The city is built atop an isolated island seperated into three teirs seprerating people into different economic classes. Trains wrap around the island acting as dividers. The top is reserved only for the most rich and powerful, only able to be accessed by exclusive gondals.

Waterways of Grogram

Digital, Procreate: More concept art for the city of Gorgram. Waterways are built into the city to be able to filter water coming from the top of the mountain down. Little regulation is put on what can be dumped and as such, the waterways are lined with waste pipes.
